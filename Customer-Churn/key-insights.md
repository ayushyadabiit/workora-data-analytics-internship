# Key Insights

## Business Insights

- Contract type strongly influences customer retention.
- Long-term contracts reduce churn significantly.
- Customer tenure is an important indicator of loyalty.
- High monthly charges may contribute to customer dissatisfaction.
- Early customer engagement can improve retention.

## Recommendations

- Encourage long-term contracts.
- Offer loyalty rewards.
- Improve customer support.
- Provide personalized retention offers.
