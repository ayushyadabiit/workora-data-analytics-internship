# Churn Prediction

## Objective

Predict whether a customer is likely to churn based on historical customer information.

## Possible Features

- Tenure
- Monthly Charges
- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Payment Method
- Senior Citizen
- Gender

## Suggested Models

- Logistic Regression
- Decision Tree
- Random Forest

## Evaluation Metrics

- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- F1 Score

## Conclusion

Machine learning can help identify customers at risk of leaving, allowing businesses to take proactive retention measures.
